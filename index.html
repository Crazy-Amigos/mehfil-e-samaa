Mehfil-e-Sama’a is a Delhi based Sufi-Hindustani classical music ensemble mostly comprised of artists from Kerala who are settled in Delhi. It has already performed in around 50 stages across India and there are active discussions going on for stages in Middle East and South Asia.

Genre

Mehfil-e-Sama’a performs genres like Sufi, Qawwali, Ghazals and Hindustani classical music. The troop itself has artists who have learned and experienced classical music in good depth.
